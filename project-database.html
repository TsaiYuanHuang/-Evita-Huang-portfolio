研究背景Context

七年的洞察，正在沉睡

Seven Years of Insight, Sitting Idle

研究團隊自 2017 年起累積多元議題的質性研究，但這些珍貴的訪談逐字稿，長期以散落的紙本或個人資料夾形式保存。

Since 2017, the research team had accumulated qualitative studies across diverse topics. But these valuable interview transcripts sat scattered across paper documents and individual folders.

每次啟動新研究，研究員都需要重新從零開始——難以快速調閱歷年洞察、無法跨研究比對、知識無法累積，造成重複投入與資源浪費。

Every new research project meant starting from scratch — unable to quickly retrieve past insights, unable to compare across studies, knowledge failing to accumulate, leading to duplicated effort and wasted resources.

這個專案的起點，是一個簡單的問題：怎麼讓過去的研究，繼續對未來有用？

The starting question was simple: how do we make past research continuously useful for the future?

設計策略Strategy

讓資料從「靜態存檔」升級為「動態洞察工具」

From Static Archive to Dynamic Insight Tool

核心策略是以研究者的實際使用需求為核心規劃資料庫架構，而非以技術可行性為出發點。整體分為三個設計層次：

The core strategy was to design the database architecture around researchers' actual usage needs — not technical feasibility. The design operated on three layers:

Layer 01

標準化資料欄位定義

Standardized Field Definition

將訪談內容拆解為五大飲食歷程情境，設計可跨研究比對的規格化欄位——讓不同年份、不同研究計畫的資料說「同一種語言」。

Decomposed interview content into five dietary journey contexts, designing standardized fields that work across studies — so data from different years and projects speaks the same language.

Layer 02

標籤化與量化

Tagging and Quantification

與資策會數位轉型研究院合作，導入 AI 技術將質性文本轉換為可計數的標籤資料——讓質性資料得以統計、視覺化，並支援跨研究的量化比較。

Collaborated with the Institute for Information Industry to introduce AI technology that transforms qualitative text into countable tag data — enabling qualitative data to be visualized and quantitatively compared across studies.

Layer 03

儀表板設計

Dashboard Design

於 Airtable Interface 建置篩選式圖表儀表板，支援研究員以人口特徵、研究主題、食用情境等多維度切入分析——讓資料不只能存，更能被快速使用。

Built a filter-based chart dashboard in Airtable Interface — enabling researchers to slice and analyze by demographic characteristics, research topics, consumption contexts, and more.

反思Reflection
核心學習Key Learning
「研究的價值不只在於產生洞察，而在於能否被持續使用與累積。」
"The value of research lies not just in generating insights — but in whether those insights can be persistently used and accumulated."

這個專案讓我意識到，研究本身也能夠產品化（productization）。過去的研究多半以報告作為最終交付，但我觀察到：即使內容有價值，仍難以在不同專案間被快速調用；知識往往隨時間與人員流動而流失。

This project made me realize that research itself can be productized. Most research ends with a report — but I observed that even valuable content is hard to retrieve across projects, and knowledge tends to erode with time and personnel changes.

這也讓我從「單一研究專案思維」轉向「設計可長期運作的資料系統」——不只是解決當下問題，而是讓資料結構能持續擴充與應用。

This shifted my thinking from "individual research project" to "designing systems that operate long-term" — not just solving today's problem, but building data structures that can continuously expand and be applied.

未來若再優化：

If I were to iterate:

  • 建立使用成效指標，追蹤資料庫實際被使用的頻率與影響
  • Establish usage metrics to track how frequently and impactfully the database is actually used
  • 持續調整標籤與 prompt，優化 AI 應用準確度
  • Continuously refine tags and prompts to improve AI application accuracy
  • 進一步串接產品開發或決策流程，深化資料應用場景
  • Further connect the database to product development or decision-making flows to deepen application scenarios
