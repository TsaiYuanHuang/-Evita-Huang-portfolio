研究背景Context

業者的困難不是「缺資料」

The Real Gap Wasn't Data — It Was Usability

在「活躍老化」趨勢下，越來越多具運動習慣的中高齡族群開始重視蛋白質攝取。但多數食品業者仍停留在「知道重要，卻不知道怎麼做產品」的階段。

As active aging becomes a mainstream trend, more middle-aged and older adults with exercise habits are paying attention to protein intake. Yet most food companies remained stuck: they knew protein mattered, but didn't know how to act on it.

業者面臨的真正困難不是「缺資料」，而是：無法理解不同族群的本質差異、難以將洞察轉化為具體產品方向、缺乏讓團隊共同理解使用者並做決策的工具。

The real challenge wasn't missing data — it was: difficulty understanding meaningful differences between user groups, no clear path from insight to product direction, and no shared tool for cross-functional teams to align on users and make decisions together.

這個專案的目標因此不只是「做研究報告」，而是設計出一套讓洞察可以被使用、被決策的工具與機制。

The goal therefore wasn't just to produce a research report — it was to design tools and mechanisms that make insights actually usable in decision-making.

從混合研究到洞察結構化

From Mixed-method Research to Structured Insight

研究透過問卷篩選與深度訪談，識別四類族群分眾，並系統性地將訪談資料轉化為結構化洞察。

The research used survey screening and in-depth interviews to identify four distinct user segments, systematically translating interview data into structured insights.

混合研究設計

Mixed-method Design

  • 問卷篩選確認樣本
  • Survey screening to confirm sample
  • 半結構式訪談大綱，聚焦飲食與運動情境中的決策脈絡
  • Semi-structured guide focused on dietary and exercise decision contexts
  • 視覺化素材引導受訪者具體化抽象偏好
  • Visual materials to help participants articulate abstract preferences

質性分析架構

Qualitative Analysis

  • 編碼架構：價值 ／ 行為 ／ 情境三大面向
  • Coding scheme: value / behavior / context dimensions
  • 逐字稿拆解為最小語意單位，提升分析精度
  • Transcripts decomposed to minimum semantic units for precision
  • 親和圖法進行資料群聚，辨識關鍵行為模式
  • Affinity mapping for data clustering and pattern identification

設計讓業者可以操作的工具

Designing Tools Non-researchers Can Use

研究分析完成後，最關鍵的挑戰是：如何讓沒有研究背景的行銷、研發、業務團隊，能夠真正理解並使用這些洞察？答案不是報告，而是工具。

After completing the analysis, the critical challenge was: how do you get marketing, R&D, and sales teams — with no research background — to genuinely understand and use these insights? The answer wasn't a report. It was tools.

工具 01Tool 01

Persona 人物誌

Persona Cards

以行為與生活脈絡為核心構建人物誌架構，而非僅依人口統計分類。四位人物誌各自描繪不同的飲食決策邏輯、運動情境與產品期待，凸顯族群間的本質差異。

Built persona frameworks around behavior and life context rather than demographics alone. Four personas each capture distinct dietary decision logic, exercise contexts, and product expectations — highlighting meaningful differences between segments.

工具 02Tool 02

行為洞察卡片

Behavioral Insight Cards

將零散的質性語料轉化為結構化卡片組，每張卡片對應一個具體的使用者行為情境與背後動機。設計目的是讓工作坊參與者能直接操作，降低從「理解洞察」到「產品發想」之間的摩擦。

Transformed fragmented qualitative data into structured card sets — each card mapping a specific user behavior scenario and underlying motivation. Designed so workshop participants can physically handle them, reducing friction between "understanding insight" and "generating product ideas."

B2B 共創工作坊B2B Co-creation Workshop

讓研究洞察驅動產品決策

Turning Insight Into Product Decisions

工作坊的設計邏輯不是「讓業者聽研究報告」，而是讓他們透過操作工具本身來理解使用者。Persona 卡和行為洞察卡在這個環境中不只是展示素材，而是引導討論、促進決策對齊的操作介面。

The workshop wasn't designed to have companies "listen to research findings" — it was designed to let them understand users by physically working with the tools. Persona cards and insight cards function not as presentation materials but as operational interfaces that guide discussion and promote decision alignment.

反思Reflection
核心學習Key Learning
「真正有價值的研究，不只是產出 insight，而是讓 insight 能被使用、被決策、被轉化為產品。」
"Valuable research isn't just about producing insights — it's about making insights usable, decisionable, and translatable into products."

這個專案讓我意識到，研究者的角色不只是「產生知識」，更是設計知識的傳遞介面。報告是一種格式，但 Persona 卡和行為卡是另一種格式——它們能讓不同背景的人在同一張桌子上，對「使用者是誰」這件事達成共識。

This project made me realize that a researcher's role isn't just to "generate knowledge" — it's to design the interface through which knowledge gets transferred. A report is one format, but persona cards and insight cards are another — they allow people from different backgrounds to reach shared understanding of "who the user is" around the same table.

未來若再優化，我會建立 workshop 成果與後續產品開發成效的長期追蹤機制，讓 insight → product KPI 的連結更加可見。

If I were to iterate, I'd establish a longitudinal tracking mechanism connecting workshop outputs to downstream product development outcomes — making the insight → product KPI link more visible.
